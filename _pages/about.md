I have always aspired to be a scientist, and that passion has been the guiding light throughout my life's journey. I find immense joy in the pursuit of knowledge and the art of research. It's not merely a profession; it's a calling.

Research, to me, isn't just a means to an end; it's a belief in the ethics of discovery, a quest for truth, and a commitment to making the world a better place. I am, at heart, a philanthropist, and my research is the canvas on which I express my commitment to humanity's well-being.

My journey in the world of science began during my undergraduate years, where I embarked on a path in cancer research. Since then, I have dedicated myself to this critical field, working in diverse labs and under various mentors. For me, cancer is like a complex puzzle waiting to be solved, and every day brings a new piece of the solution.

Beyond the laboratory, I have a diverse range of interests. I find solace in the written word, the strokes of a paintbrush on canvas, and the thrill of exploration through travel. Movies are my escape, allowing me to dive into different worlds and narratives, inspiring creativity in my own work.

This website is a glimpse into my multifaceted world—a world where science, philanthropy, and creativity intersect. Join me on this journey of discovery, as we explore the intricacies of research, share insights, and celebrate the beauty of science and the arts.

Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to sharing this incredible voyage with you.
