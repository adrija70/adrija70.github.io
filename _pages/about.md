I am an aspiring researcher with a focused interest on cancer biology, more attuned to chemoresistance, epithelial to mesenchymal transition (EMT), and metastasis. 
I completed my Bachelor of Technology (BTech) in Biotechnology from Haldia Institute of Technology. I completed my Master of Technology (MTech) in Biological Sciences and Bioengineering from Indian Institute of Kanpur.


The major projects that I have contributed to involve screening biomolecular targets with significant regulatory potential in diseased state combined with screening drug repositories for suitable drugs that can be repurposed to target the identified biomolecules. 


I am interested in advancing the understanding of complex cancer biology, with a particular focus on uncovering the mechanisms behind chemoresistance, epithelial-to-mesenchymal transition, and metastasis. My goal is to contribute to the development of targeted strategies that can effectively combat these challenging aspects of cancer progression.
