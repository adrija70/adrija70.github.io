Designed and executed a study targeting epithelial-to-mesenchymal transition (EMT) to combat chemoresistance and metastasis, with a focus on Snail as a key EMT transcription factor. Used fragment-based screening and molecular docking, identifying Remdesivir as a high-affinity Snail inhibitor.

Validated Remdesivir’s inhibitory effects on EMT through in vitro assays, observing changes in EMT markers, invasion, migration, and colony formation. 


Combined Remdesivir with chemotherapy to address both tumor growth and metastasis, achieving synergistic cytotoxicity at nanomolar concentrations, which may reduce chemotherapy’s side effects.


Conducted in vitro validations of the combination therapy in 4T1, PC3, and MDA-MB-231 followed by in vivo validation.
